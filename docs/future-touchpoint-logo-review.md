# Future Touchpoint Logo Review

The current Touchpoint logo should remain in place for now because the client has not approved a logo replacement.

This note documents why the mark should be reviewed later as a separate brand exercise, not as part of the immediate website polish pass.

## Why review it later

The supplied Touchpoint materials position the brand as a serious product under the New Wave Synergy umbrella:

- Touchpoint by New Wave Synergy™;
- Post-Completion, Handover & Asset Transition Platform;
- AU + NZ operating region;
- built-environment, handover, asset transition and operational-readiness focus;
- a professional product for developers, builders, asset owners, facilities managers, purchasers and end users.

The product mark should eventually support that positioning with the same level of clarity, confidence and professional polish.

## Current concerns to review

Do not present these as criticism of the existing work. Present them as normal brand-system refinement points:

- The current mark may not scale cleanly across every touchpoint, especially small mobile headers, favicon/app-icon contexts, sales decks and printed collateral.
- The relationship between Touchpoint and New Wave Synergy should be locked up more consistently so the product clearly sits under the NWS brand umbrella.
- The mark should feel suitable for a serious built-environment platform, not just a generic SaaS/app logo.
- The identity system should work across website, capability statement, pitch decks, product UI, QR/NFC tags, email signatures and social profiles.
- The mark should be tested in light mode, dark mode, monochrome, small sizes and print.

## Recommended future direction

A later logo/brand exercise should create:

- a primary Touchpoint by New Wave Synergy lockup;
- a compact product mark for app/icon/favicons;
- a mono version;
- light and dark versions;
- minimum-size and spacing rules;
- usage examples for web, decks, QR/NFC tags and capability statements;
- a clear parent-brand relationship to New Wave Synergy.

## Client-safe wording

Suggested framing:

> The current logo can remain in place for this website pass. Longer term, Touchpoint is becoming a serious product under the New Wave Synergy umbrella, so it would benefit from a dedicated logo/lockup refinement. The goal would not be to throw away the brand, but to make sure it scales cleanly across the website, product UI, pitch decks, printed collateral, QR/NFC tags, and small mobile contexts while clearly showing the New Wave Synergy relationship.
